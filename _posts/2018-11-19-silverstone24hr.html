Citroen C1 Silverstone 24hr

April 26th-28th 2019

Majestic Motorsport are pleased to announce our entry into the inaugural Citroen C1 Silverstone 24hr race, to take place 26th to 28th April, 2019.

We will be offering arrive and drive packages for this event, based on 5 or 6 drivers sharing. Early booking is recommended, drop us a line at info@majestic-motorsport.co.uk, call us on 01926 658923.
