Taster Days

Try out a Citroen C1 race car

If you're unsure whether or not Citroen C1 racing is for you, why not come along to one of our taster days?

They're an opportunity to try out a car, get some tuition and meet our team, all in the familiar environment of a regular track day. You don't need any previous race experience, or a race licence, just turn up and let us guide you through your first steps.

Up to four people can share the car on the day, and our instructors will be on hand to help you get to deploy all 67bhp as efficiently as possible to the race track. Fuel, tyres, refreshments and light snacks are all included, and advice about how to start racing is free.
