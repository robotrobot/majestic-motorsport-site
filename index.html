Majestic Motorsport

Citroen C1 endurance racing team

Interested in Citroen C1 Racing in 2019? Silverstone 24hr, Spa or other UK rounds? We can help. Our services include arrive and drive, C1 racecar builds or running a car for you and your team for a whole season.

If you're not sure racing is for you, we even hold taster days where you can try out a race car with no commitment.

About Us

We're a friendly yet professional and well organised bunch. In the past we've crewed works race teams at Le Mans, raced in British GT and Superbikes, worked in senior engineering roles for various automotive manufacturers and even restored a classic car or two.

We're probably not the cheapest option in the paddock, but if you value a well prepared quick car, the backup of experienced racers and mechanics, and that in endurance racing you need to first finish to finish first, you'll understand we offer excellent value.
